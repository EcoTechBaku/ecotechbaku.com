About us

What is EcoTech?

EcoTech is a 2-day research exhibition organized by the youth for the youth that will be held on 9-10th of September.
The first day of the event focuses on sustainability exhibits, whilst the second day focuses on technology-related projects.

Why apply to EcoTech

By applying to EcoTech, you will have the opportunity to become a part of this exhibition combining the worlds of Sustainability and technology. By connecting with a diverse group of individuals, participants will build a strong professional network, exchange ideas, and develop lifelong friendships.

EcoTech provides a valuable platform for participants to gain recognition for their work and ideas. Winning or even showcasing their ideas at the Baku EcoTech Research Exhibiton 2023 can lead to media coverage, awards, university scholarships, thereby boosting their profile and increasing their potential for future success in their academic careers.

Joining EcoTech provides an excellent platform for young minds to explore and develop innovative solutions that integrate technology and ecology. We hope this event encourages everyone to shift their perspective in how we look and ecology and technology.

What is the difference of group and individual application?

Group applications are considered for the participants who have established a group before applying to EcoTech and brainstormed a project idea for this exhibition together. It’s expected for all the group members’ names to be mentioned in the application form for them to get credit for their project.
